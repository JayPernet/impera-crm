# UX Principles & Best Practices

## Visual Hierarchy
- **Size:** Most important elements should be largest.
- **Color:** Use the 60-30-10 rule (60% Neutral, 30% Secondary, 10% Accent).
- **Whitespace:** It's not empty space; it's an active design element. Use 8pt grid (spacing 4, 8, 16, 24...).

## Gestalt Principles
- **Proximity:** Related items should be close together.
- **Similarity:** Elements with same function should look alike.

## Accessibility (A11y)
- **Contrast:** Text vs Background must meet 4.5:1 ratio (AA).
- **Focus:** Never remove outline on focus unless replacing it with custom focus styles.
- **Motion:** Respect `prefers-reduced-motion` media query.

## Anti-Patterns & Freshness (StarIAup Standard)
- **Avoid "Standard" Gradients:** Purple-to-Blue is overused. Explore organic earth tones, deep monochromatic scales, or high-contrast vibrant pairs that aren't the tech-startup default.
- **Avoid "Standard" Fonts:** Inter and Plus Jakarta Sans are the "Helvetica" and "Montserrat" of the 2026s. Unless requested for utility/blandness, do not use them.
